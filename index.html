Procedural Dungeon Crawler

This is a small project where I explored procedural generation and Behaviour tree's and how to tie it all together with a turn based system!

There is also an drag and drop Inventory System!

For the procedural generation I make use of a custom grid system. On it I randomly spawn rooms with variable dimensions.

For the paths/hallways I make use of Delaunay Triangulation and use a Minimal spanning tree to find 1 continuos path through the triangulation mesh. And readd a few of the edges to make a loop in the level.

Finally I use the A* pathfinding to traverse the grid and place the floor & wall tiles!

Custom Behaviour tree editor in Unity
